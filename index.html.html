Hajime no Ippo

16 year old high schooler Makunouchi Ippo is on his way home until he is suddenly ganged up on by a group of 3 bullies. In the midst of his beating, a boxer comes to his aid and is soon brought to a boxing gym. After being offered to hit the bag and unbeknownst to him, that day would change the rest of his life moving forward.
